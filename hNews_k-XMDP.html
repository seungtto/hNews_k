class

HTML4 definition of the 'class' attribute. This meta data profile defines some 'class' attribute values (class names) and their meanings as suggested by a draft of "Hypertext Links in HTML".

entry-title
Used to describe the title of news stories.
entry-content
Used to describe the content of news stories.
source-org
The originating organization for the news story.
writer
The name of person who wrote the news story.
dateline
The date when the news story was filed or modified.
rel
source
Represents the source of the story.
principles
Represents the statement of principles and ethics of news organization.
